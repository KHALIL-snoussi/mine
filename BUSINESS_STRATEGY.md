## ğŸ­ Operations & Fulfillment

### Inventory Strategy

**3-Tier Approach:**
1. **Just-in-Time:** Starter kits (low volume)
2. **Safety Stock:** Creative kits (high volume)
3. **Bulk Order:** Professional kits (quarterly)

**Suppliers:**
- Paints: Bulk acrylic from China ($0.30/bottle)
- Brushes: Vietnam ($0.40/brush)
- Packaging: Local (faster, better quality)

**Warehouse:**
- Month 1-6: Garage/spare room
- Month 7-12: 3PL (Third-party logistics)
- Target: < 5% stockout rate

### Fulfillment

**In-House (Months 1-3):**
- Pack and ship yourself
- 2-3 day turnaround
- Cost: Time + $4 shipping

**3PL (Months 4+):**
- Partner with ShipBob or similar
- Same-day fulfillment
- Cost: $3/pick + $0.50/item + shipping
