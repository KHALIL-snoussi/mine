## ğŸš¨ Biggest Risks & How to Mitigate

### Risk 1: No One Buys
**Why it happens:** Product-market fit doesn't exist
**Mitigation:**
- Validate with 10 friends/family first
- Interview 5 target customers before launch
- Offer money-back guarantee
- Start with low prices ($24.99) to reduce barrier

### Risk 2: High Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
**Why it happens:** Ad targeting is wrong, conversion is low
**Mitigation:**
- Track CAC from day 1 (goal: <$30)
- Test multiple ad variations
- Focus on organic first (content, community)
- Optimize conversion rate (every 1% matters)

### Risk 3: Quality Issues (Paint/Templates)
**Why it happens:** Rushed sourcing, bad suppliers
**Mitigation:**
- Order samples from 3 suppliers
- Test yourself before selling
- Start with one kit variant
- QA every order initially
- Great customer service = forgiveness

### Risk 4: Can't Keep Up with Orders
**Why it happens:** Success! (Good problem)
**Mitigation:**
- Start with "pre-order" model (ship in 5-7 days)
- Use 3PL once you hit 50+ orders/month
- Automate template generation
- Hire VA for customer support early

### Risk 5: Run Out of Money
**Why it happens:** Burn rate too high, revenue too slow
**Mitigation:**
- Bootstrap as long as possible
- Reinvest profits, don't spend personal savings
- Keep day job until $5K/month revenue
- Only raise money after proving traction
